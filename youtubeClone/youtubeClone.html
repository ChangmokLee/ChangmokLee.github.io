views 88,977 2023.2.9 Ingredients: 1 lb of dried spaghetti or any other white pasta 4 quarts of water 2 tbsp of salt 4 tbsp of unsalted butter 1 cup of heavy cream 1 tsp of freshly ground black pepper 1/2 tsp of garlic powder (optional) 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese 2 tbsp of freshly chopped parsley (optional) Instructions: Fill a large pot with 4 quarts of water and add 2 tbsp of salt. Place the pot over high heat and bring to a boil. Once the water is boiling, add the pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes or until al dente. Drain the pasta and reserve 1 cup of the pasta water. In the same pot, melt 4 tbsp of unsalted butter over medium heat. Add 1 cup of heavy cream and stir to combine. Season the sauce with 1 tsp of freshly ground black pepper and 1/2 tsp of garlic powder (optional). Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly. If the sauce is too thick, add some of the reserved pasta water to thin it out. Turn off the heat and stir in 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese. Serve the pasta in individual bowls and sprinkle with 2 tbsp of freshly chopped parsley (optional). Enjoy your delicious white pasta!
